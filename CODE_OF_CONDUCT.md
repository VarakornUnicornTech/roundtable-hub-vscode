# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in RoundTable Hub a respectful, professional, and inclusive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behaviors:**
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory remarks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam or disruptive behavior

## AI Contributions

AI-generated contributions are welcome and held to the same standards as human contributions. AI content must be disclosed.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to [@VarakornUnicornTech](https://github.com/VarakornUnicornTech). All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.
